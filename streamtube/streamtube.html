How to install


First, enable installation of apps from unkown sources on your Android device, by going to the Settings, followed by Security and enabling the option "Unkown sources".


Next, go to this page on your Android device, and click the "Download" button at the top of this page. Finally, tap on the downloaded apk file to start the installation process and follow the instructions on the device.
